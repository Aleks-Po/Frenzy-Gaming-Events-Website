About Frenzy Events

We organise competitive gaming events, bringing together players, communities and esports experiences across the UK.

Who We Are

Frenzy Events is a team dedicated to organising high-quality gaming and esports events. We focus on creating engaging experiences for both players and spectators.

Our goal is to deliver professionally managed competitions, accessible venues and exciting live environments for the gaming community.
