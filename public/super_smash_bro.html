About This Competition

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is a fast-paced fighting game where iconic characters battle in intense matches. Players use unique moves and smart strategies to knock opponents off the stage and win.

This competition features a 1v1 format with 32 players competing for the top spot. Matches are played as Best of 3 during the playoffs and Best of 5 in the grand final. Each game is set to 3 stocks with an 8-minute time limit, with items and stage hazards turned off for fair play.

The competition is highly exciting, testing players’ speed, timing, and skill. It is popular among both casual players and competitive esports fans.

Alongside that the competition comes with high-energy battles and crowd-favourite characters, this competition delivers nonstop action and entertainment. Every match is full of surprises, making it exciting for both dedicated gamers and first-time viewers.

The total prize pool is £5000, rewarding the top three players. This event promises intense action, thrilling moments, and a great experience for both players and spectators.
